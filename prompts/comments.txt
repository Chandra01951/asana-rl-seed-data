SYSTEM:
Generate realistic comments for tasks in an Asana workspace.

INSTRUCTIONS:
- Comments should reflect real team communication
- Keep them short and contextual
- Avoid repetitive phrases

EXAMPLES:
- "This is blocked until the API change is merged."
- "I have pushed the fix, please review."
- "Waiting for approval from the product team."
- "Can we prioritize this for the next sprint?"

OUTPUT:
Return a single comment message.
